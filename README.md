# 🎓 CollegeAdmitIQ

AI-powered Android app that helps high school students build
a strong college portfolio to get into their dream college.

## ✨ Features

- 📊 **Portfolio Tracker** — Log activities, awards, volunteering,
  leadership, research and more
- 🤖 **AI Advisor** — Gemini AI suggests personalized activities
  based on your major, interests and dream colleges
- 📈 **Gap Analyzer** — AI identifies exactly what's missing
  in your portfolio for your dream colleges
- 🗂️ **Category Coverage** — Track all 10 Common App categories
- ⚙️ **Profile Settings** — Update your profile, SAT/ACT scores,
  interests and dream colleges anytime
- 📅 **Year by Year** — Tailored for 9th–12th grade students

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **UI** — Jetpack Compose
- **AI** — Google Gemini API
- **Architecture** — MVVM + StateFlow
- **DI** — Hilt
- **Database** — Room
- **Async** — Kotlin Coroutines + Flow
- **Navigation** — Jetpack Navigation Compose
